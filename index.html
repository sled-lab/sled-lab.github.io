Systems & Learning for Edge Devices

SLED Lab, the Systems & Learning for Edge Devices Laboratory, explores where system software meets the physical world — processors, microarchitecture, batteries, power, and heat. We replace hand-tuned rules with learning, domain expertise, and scheduling theory, validated on edge devices from smartphones to electric vehicles.

We are always looking for motivated students to join us — please see Contact.

Research areas

A. Learning-based system management
B. Battery/power management in mobile systems
C. System-level supports for mobile user experience
D. Novel scheduling algorithm & framework
